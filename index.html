CSC Branches is CSC's initiative to further develop the SCI community and foster more niche interests within. Through this program, CSC members are able to easily create and manage their own communities (called "Branches") by forgoing SCI and SORC bureaucracy via CSC. This allows club founders, open source projects, and small interest groups to focus less on monotonous paperwork and slow processes, and more on what matters - their community, passions, missions, and members.
